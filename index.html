2013 — 2016

CEO & cofounder at KptnCook

KptnCook is a mobile service making grocery shopping decisions and cooking process easier. KptnCook app matches great recipes with products and prices from local grocery stores.

 

Back in 2013 I had an idea how to disrupt the food industry, I pitched it at the Startup Weekend. In 54 hours we had our business model and app prototype ready. We won the first prize and KptnCook was born.

 

KptnCook App was featured multiple times by Apple in #1 spot for food and drinks and as Editor's Choice Best New App in several countries on iPhone and iPad App Store.

2012 — 2013

Junior Product Manager at Mirapodo

Mirapodo is one of the leading German fashion e-commerce shops, specializing on shoes and accessories.

 

At Mirapodo my main project was a shoe fitting and sizing tool. I was cooperating with internal stakeholders and an agency which was responsible for the technology. I performed A/B tests and customer surveys in order to monitor the impact for customers and business, i.e. decrease the returns.

 

As a result of my detailed analysis we were able to detect the issues of the tool which were overseen in the past. I was able to persuade the team to test other tools on the market.
